## Returns

- If an item arrives **damaged, incorrect, or missing**, contact us within **48 hours** of delivery so we can make it right.
- Sealed products are generally eligible for return **only if unopened** and in original condition.
- The buyer is responsible for return shipping unless the return is due to our error.
- Please contact support **before** returning an item so we can provide proper instructions.

---

## Refunds

Once a return is received and inspected, refunds are issued back to the original payment method.  
Processing times vary depending on your bank or card issuer.
