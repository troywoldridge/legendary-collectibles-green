# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Legendary Collectibles.

---

## Do you guarantee authenticity?

Yes. We stand behind what we sell. If you ever have a concern about an item, contact us immediately.

---

## How are singles packaged?

Singles are protected using penny sleeves, top loaders, or equivalent protection, then packed to prevent bending and moisture damage.

---

## Do you combine shipping?

Usually, yes. When multiple items ship together, we do our best to consolidate shipments and keep shipping costs reasonable.

---

## How do returns work?

Please see our **Shipping & Returns** policy for details.  
If something arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us as soon as possible so we can help.

---

## Can I cancel an order?

If your order hasn’t shipped yet, contact us right away and we’ll try to stop it.  
Once an order has shipped, returns follow our standard return policy.

---

## Do you sell graded cards?

Yes. Listings clearly state the grading company (PSA, BGS, CGC, etc.) and the grade.

---

## I have a question that isn’t answered here

Email **support@legendary-collectibles.com** and we’ll be happy to help.
