# About Legendary Collectibles

Legendary Collectibles is a modern destination for trading cards and collectibles — built by collectors, for collectors. We focus on clean presentation, transparent context, and careful handling so you can shop and explore with confidence.

---

## Collector-First, Retail-Ready

We’re here for the fun part of the hobby: discovering new sets, hunting favorites, and building collections you’re proud of. Alongside research tools, we offer **sealed TCG products**, **singles**, **graded cards**, and **accessories**.

Inventory is sourced through **authorized distribution** and trusted partners whenever possible, with an emphasis on authenticity and clear policies.

---

## Our Mission

Collecting should be **fun, transparent, and accessible**.

- **Discover faster:** clean navigation, smart filters, and organized set indexes
- **Research smarter:** pricing context and metadata where available
- **Shop confidently:** clear condition notes and careful packaging

---

## What We Carry

- **TCG:** Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Magic: The Gathering (with more games planned)
- **Sports Cards:** Baseball, Basketball, Football
- **Collectibles:** figures and pop-culture items (expanding)
- **Accessories:** sleeves, binders, storage, and display solutions

## Pricing & Data Transparency

When available, we display pricing snapshots and references gathered from reputable sources and marketplaces.  
Prices are informational only and may change quickly.

Nothing on this site constitutes financial or investment advice.

---

## Condition, Grading & Authenticity

- Listings are described as clearly and accurately as possible.
- Graded and raw cards are labeled distinctly.
- We do not grade cards ourselves.
- Third-party grading and certification should always be verified with the grading company.

---

## What We Are Not

- We are not a grading company.
- We do not provide investment guarantees.
- We do not guarantee future collectible value.
